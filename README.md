Project idea:
Ecounity is an innovative company that combines recycling with artificial intelligence. Our mission is to create a smarter, cleaner, and more sustainable world. We use AI-powered systems to identify, sort, and process recyclable materials efficiently. Smart sensors and machine learning help reduce human error and waste contamination. Our technology improves recycling rates while lowering costs for communities and industries. EcoUnity also educates people about sustainable habits through interactive AI platforms. By merging technology and environmental care, we make recycling faster and smarter. Together, we build a unified future where technology serves the planet.
